The Primary Pathway

From early phonics to advanced Year 6 reasoning, we cover the entire national curriculum and beyond.

Key Stage 1 (Ages 5-7)

The crucial early years. We focus heavily on phonics, reading comprehension, and foundational numeracy. Our tutors make learning feel like a game, fostering a lifelong love of education.

  • Interactive Phonics & Spelling
  • Early Number Bonds & Basic Arithmetic
  • Confidence Building & Expression

Key Stage 2 (Ages 7-11)

Moving from learning to read, to reading to learn. We introduce complex problem-solving, advanced grammar, and core scientific principles to prepare them for SATs and secondary school.

  • Year 6 SATs Preparation
  • Fractions, Decimals & Applied Maths
  • Advanced Creative Writing

Plus: Coding for Young Minds

In today's digital world, coding is as essential as reading. We offer optional add-on modules for primary students to learn logic, problem-solving, and basic programming using Scratch and Python in a highly visual, gamified environment.
